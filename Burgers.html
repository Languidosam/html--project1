• THESE ARE THE FIVE CATEGORIES OF KRABBY PATTY

  • Classic Krabby

    • The Classic Krabby Patty is the quintessential burger that everyone in Bikini Bottom knows and loves.
    It's the perfect combination of fresh ingredients, brought together by the closely guarded secret formula that has kept customers coming back for years.
    The Classic Krabby Patty features a juicy, tender patty cooked to perfection and topped with crisp lettuce, a slice of cheddar cheese, crunchy pickles,
    finely chopped onions, a dollop of ketchup, and a smear of mustard, all nestled between a toasted, golden sesame seed bun.


  • Jelly Patty

    • The Jelly Patty is a unique twist on the traditional Krabby Patty, featuring a sweet and tangy topping that sets it apart.
    This variant includes the famous jellyfish jelly, a vibrant, glowing purple spread that adds an unexpected flavor explosion with every bite.
    Introduced in the memorable episode "Jellyfish Jam," the Jelly Patty offers a playful contrast between the savory patty and the sweet jelly, making it a hit with those looking for something different.
    The jelly adds a sticky, sugary layer to the burger, enhancing the flavor profile and creating a truly unique eating experience.

  • Kelp Delight

    • For the health-conscious residents of Bikini Bottom, the Kelp Delight is the perfect choice.
    This vegetarian version of the Krabby Patty replaces the traditional meat patty with a kelp-based alternative that’s both nutritious and flavorful.
    The Kelp Patty has a distinctive green hue and a slightly earthy taste, reminiscent of the ocean’s natural bounty.
    Accompanied by kelp lettuce, seaweed strips, and a drizzle of tangy seaweed sauce, this burger is packed with vitamins and minerals that make it as healthy as it is tasty.
    The Kelp Delight offers a refreshing take on the Krabby Patty, catering to those who prefer a plant-based diet without sacrificing the deliciousness of a classic burger.

  • Super Spicy Patty

    • For those who crave a bit of heat, the Super Spicy Patty is the ultimate choice.
    This fiery version of the Krabby Patty is not for the faint of heart.
    The patty is infused with a blend of the hottest sea peppers, giving it a kick that’s sure to make your taste buds tingle.
    Topped with a spicy coral sauce that drips tantalizingly from the sides, this burger also includes the usual lettuce, tomato, and onions,
    but with an added layer of sliced hot sea cucumbers for extra crunch and spice.


  • Bubble Bass

    • Inspired by the character Bubble Bass, the Bubble Bass Deluxe is a burger that’s as big and bold as its namesake.
    This towering Krabby Patty is double-stacked with two juicy patties, making it twice as satisfying.
    Each layer is packed with all the classic toppings:
    extra pickles (a must-have for Bubble Bass), melted cheese, crisp lettuce, fresh tomato slices, and onions, all held together by a sturdy, sesame-seed bun.
    The Bubble Bass Deluxe is a meal in itself, offering a heartier and more indulgent version of the classic Krabby Patty.
